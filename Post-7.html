What to Keep in Mind When Buying a Gaming Laptop for Less Than $1,000:

Graphics Performance: Look for laptops with NVIDIA RTX 4050 or better GPUs to get the best gaming experience.

Processor: New CPUs like the AMD Ryzen 5 7640HS or Intel Core i7-13620H strike a good balance between how well they work and how much power they use.

Display: A screen that refreshes quickly (144 Hz or more) makes games look better ones that move fast.

RAM and Storage: You'll want at least 16GB of RAM and a 512GB SSD to run many programs at once and load games .

Keep an eye out for sales and price cuts, as costs can change during big shopping events. Also, think about whether you can upgrade parts later to make the laptop last longer.
