Amazon Prime Games

Prime Gaming is a gaming service which is a part of Amazon Prime which can be accessed by taking Prime membership. It offers a variety of games. Here’s a bunch of information:

Key Features of Prime Gaming:

  • Free Games: A member can access games free of cost. Once you install the game, it will be accessible forever.
  • Game Trials: Early access for some selected games.
  • In-game Content: Get free in-game loot, such as characters, currency, skins, and other items for popular games like Apex Legends, Roblox, Valorant, and more.

Steps to Access Amazon Prime Gaming:

  1. Sign Up/Sign In on Amazon Prime: If you’re not a member, subscribe to Amazon Prime.
  2. Visit the Prime Gaming Website: Go to gaming.amazon.com.
  3. Link Your Accounts: Connect your Amazon account to platforms like Twitch, Epic Games, or other game services to get rewards.
  4. Claim Your Perks: Browse the available games, in-game content, and other offers, and claim them.

Is It Worth It or Not?

If you're a gamer and already have an Amazon Prime membership, Prime Gaming adds significant value and gives many games free of cost whose prices are much higher. The free games, in-game loot, and Twitch benefits make it a great perk for gamers.
